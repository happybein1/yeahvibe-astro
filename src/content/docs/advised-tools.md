## Turn insights into action

Knowledge alone isn't enough. To make better decisions, we need practical ways to apply insights.

YeahVibe curates tools — some built in-house, others freely available from trusted third parties — that help individuals and communities plan, prioritize, and act with clarity.

---

## 🧭 Prioritization

Prioritization is the foundation of effective decision-making. By ranking tasks and goals, you can focus on what truly matters instead of being overwhelmed by competing demands.

- **[YeahVibe Prioritization Notion Template](https://www.notion.so/PrioStorm-2c0fcec352808007bfd5d853e9f7f9a1)** — our in-house tool to help you rank actions by importance.
- **[Measure Your Wellbeing](https://form.jotform.com/222552689387066)** — quantify how you feel for increased awareness and to track progress over time.

## 📓 Reflection

Learning from past decisions is key to growth. Reflection tools help you track reasoning, outcomes, and lessons learned — creating a feedback loop for continuous improvement.

### AI reflection partners
Use AI to talk through decisions, explore feelings, or get a fresh perspective:

- [Copilot](https://copilot.microsoft.com/) (Microsoft)
- [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/) (Google)
- [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/) (OpenAI)
- [Mistral AI](https://mistral.ai/)
- DeepSeek

### Journaling tools
A regular journaling practice supports self-awareness and reflection:

- A simple notebook — nothing beats pen and paper for thinking clearly.
- [Journal app for Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lukasjuranek.journal) — lightweight and distraction-free.
- [Notion Journal Template](https://www.notion.so/Journal-Template-2f6fcec352808175b342d52d9f341b14) — structured journaling in Notion.
- [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/) — a powerful local note-taking tool, great for building a personal knowledge base.
- [OneNote](https://www.onenote.com/) — Microsoft's free note-taking app, well-integrated with Office tools.

## Using tools alongside insights

Tools are most powerful when paired with the frameworks in the YeahVibe knowledge base. A suggested workflow:

1. **Read** the relevant insight article to understand the concept.
2. **Apply** the appropriate tool to put it into practice.
3. **Reflect** on the outcome using journaling or AI conversation.
4. **Adapt** your approach based on what you learned.

This cycle — insight → action → reflection → adaptation — is at the heart of the [YeahVibe framework](/start-here).
