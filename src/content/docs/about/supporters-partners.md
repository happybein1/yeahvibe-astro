YeahVibe is built with the belief that better decision-making tools should be accessible to everyone. That takes a community.

## Why support YeahVibe?

If you believe that helping people make better decisions — in their personal lives, their organizations, and as citizens — creates real value in the world, then YeahVibe's mission aligns with yours.

Supporting YeahVibe means:
- Helping keep the knowledge base free and openly accessible
- Enabling the development of new tools and frameworks
- Contributing to a community of people committed to thoughtful, effective action
- Being part of a project that connects personal growth to broader societal improvement

## How you can support

### Share it
The simplest form of support: share YeahVibe with someone who would benefit. Word of mouth from people who actually use and value the platform is the most powerful growth engine we have.

### Give feedback
Good feedback is invaluable. If something is unclear, missing, wrong, or could be better — please tell us. It takes one minute and directly improves the platform for everyone.

👉 [One Minute Feedback Form](https://form.jotform.com/222551513307043)

### Join the community
Our Discord server is where the YeahVibe community gathers — to discuss ideas, share experiences, ask questions, and support each other.

👉 [Join our Discord Server](https://discord.com/invite/62xRJHCbRW)

### Partner with us
If you represent an organization — a company, NGO, educational institution, or community — and you see alignment between your mission and YeahVibe's, we'd love to explore how we can work together.

Potential partnership areas include:
- Co-creating content for specific domains or audiences
- Integrating YeahVibe frameworks into your training or development programs
- Supporting the platform financially in exchange for visibility and co-branding
- Translating content into additional languages

To discuss a partnership, reach out via Discord or the feedback form.
